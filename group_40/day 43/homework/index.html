About

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Al-Nassr and the Portugal national team. He is widely considered one of the greatest footballers of all time.

Career

Sporting CP

Ronaldo began his senior club career playing for Sporting CP, before signing with Manchester United in 2003, winning the FA Cup in his first season.

Manchester United

He won three Premier League titles, the Champions League, and the FIFA Club World Cup.

Real Madrid

He is the club's all-time top goalscorer and won four Champions League titles, three FIFA Club World Cups, and two La Liga titles.

Juventus

He won two Serie A titles, and a Coppa Italia.

Al-Nassr

Currently playing for Al-Nassr in the Saudi Pro League.

Achievements

  • 5 Ballon d'Or awards
  • 4 European Golden Shoes
  • 7 league titles
  • 5 UEFA Champions League titles
  • Over 800 career goals
